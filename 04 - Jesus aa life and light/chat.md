# Light and Life in Scripture

## Words, Passages, and Christian Thought

**Primary Text:**

> **John 1:4–5** —
> “In him was life, and the life was the light of men.
> The light shines in the darkness, and the darkness has not overcome it.”

This theme of **light** and **life** runs from Genesis to Revelation and is foundational to biblical theology.

---

# 1) Key Hebrew and Greek Words

## Hebrew (Old Testament)

### **Light — אוֹר (ʾôr)**

**Meaning**

* Light
* Illumination
* Brightness
* Often symbolic of God's presence and salvation

**Key Uses**

* Genesis 1:3 — “Let there be light”
* Psalm 27:1 — “The LORD is my light”
* Isaiah 60:19 — God as everlasting light

---

### **Life — חַיִּים (ḥayyîm)**

From root **חיה (ḥayah)** — "to live"

**Meaning**

* Life
* Vitality
* Living existence
* Often covenantal blessing

**Key Use**

* Deuteronomy 30:20 — “For he is your life”

---

### **Breath of Life — נְשָׁמָה (neshamah)**

**Meaning**

* Breath
* Life-giving breath from God

**Key Use**

* Genesis 2:7 — God breathes life into Adam

---

## Greek (New Testament)

### **Light — φῶς (phōs)**

**Meaning**

* Light
* Truth
* Revelation
* Moral purity

Used heavily in the Gospel of John and 1 John.

---

### **Life — ζωή (zōē)**

**Meaning**

* Divine life
* Spiritual life
* Eternal life

**Important distinction**

* **ζωή (zōē)** — divine/spiritual life
* **βίος (bios)** — biological life

---

### **Word — λόγος (logos)**

**Meaning**

* Word
* Reason
* Divine expression

Key in John 1.

---

### **Spirit — πνεῦμα (pneuma)**

**Meaning**

* Spirit
* Breath
* Wind

Often associated with giving life.

---

# 4) The Relationship Between **Light and Life**

Scripture consistently connects these two ideas.

They are not separate themes.

They are deeply unified.

---

## Foundational Linking Passage

**Psalm 36:9**

> “With you is the fountain of life;
> in your light do we see light.”

Pattern:

* Life originates in God
* Light reveals God

---

## John’s Theology

**John 1:4**

Key structure:

* **Life (zōē)** = Divine essence
* **Light (phōs)** = Divine revelation

Life produces light.

Light reveals life.

---

## Creation Pattern

Genesis 1 sequence:

1. Light created first
2. Life follows

John 1 sequence:

1. Word
2. Life
3. Light

Strong theological parallel.

---

# 5) Christian Thought on Light and Life

## Early Church

### Athanasius (4th century)

Taught:

* Christ restores life by bringing light.
* Darkness represents corruption and death.
* Light restores creation.

Work:

* *On the Incarnation*

---

### Augustine (4th–5th century)

Taught:

Light represents:

* Truth
* Knowledge of God
* Illumination of the soul

Key idea:

* Ignorance = darkness
* Knowledge of God = light
* Union with God = life

---

## Reformation

### John Calvin

Connected:

* Light → revelation
* Life → regeneration

The Spirit provides:

* Illumination
* New life

---

# 6) A Repeating Biblical Pattern

Across Scripture:

1. God gives **life**
2. Life produces **light**
3. Light defeats **darkness**
4. Darkness represents **death**

Seen in:

* Genesis 1
* Psalm 36
* John 1
* Revelation 21–22
