## Five Core Questions Compared


| Symbol | Meaning                                                                                               |
| ------ | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| ✓      | Personal relationship with God through Christ as adopted children of God                              |
| ~      | Relationship with God/divine reality exists, but not the Christian concept of adoption through Christ |
| ✗      | No personal Creator-God relationship as a central doctrine                                            |

## The Five Questions

1. **Who is God?**
2. **What is humanity's problem?**
3. **How is salvation or liberation achieved?**
4. **Who is Jesus?**
5. **What is humanity's ultimate destiny?**
