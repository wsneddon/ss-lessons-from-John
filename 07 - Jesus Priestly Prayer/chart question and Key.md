## Five Core Questions Compared

| Faith        | God                                  | Humanity's Problem                                                         | Salvation / Liberation                       | Jesus                                      | Ultimate Destiny                         | Rel. |
| ------------ | ------------------------------------ | -------------------------------------------------------------------------- | -------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------ | ---------------------------------------- | ---- |
| Judaism      | One covenant God (YHWH)              | Sin; covenant unfaithfulness                                               | Repentance, Torah, God's mercy               | Messiah not yet come                       | World to Come; resurrection (views vary) | ~    |
| Christianity | One God: Father, Son, Spirit         | Sin and separation from God                                                | Grace through faith in Christ; new birth     | Son of God, Savior, Lord                   | Eternal life with God; resurrection      | ✓    |
| Islam        | One God (Allah)                      | Disobedience to Allah                                                      | Submission, faith, good deeds, mercy         | Prophet and Messiah, not divine            | Paradise or Hell after judgment          | ~    |
| Hinduism     | Brahman; many manifestations/deities | Ignorance (*avidya*) and karma                                             | Dharma, devotion (*bhakti*), knowledge, yoga | Teacher/avatar (varies), not God incarnate | Rebirth until *moksha*                   | ~    |
| Buddhism     | No creator God                       | Ignorance (*avidya*), craving (*tanha*), resulting in suffering (*dukkha*) | Eightfold Path, wisdom, meditation           | Enlightened teacher                        | *Nirvana*; end of rebirth                | ✗    |
| Sikhism      | One God (*Waheguru*)                 | Separation from God through ego (*haumai*)                                 | God's grace, devotion, remembrance of God    | Great teacher; not divine                  | Union with God; freedom from rebirth     | ~    |

## Key

| Symbol | Meaning                                                                                               |
| ------ | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| ✓      | Personal relationship with God through Christ as adopted children of God                              |
| ~      | Relationship with God/divine reality exists, but not the Christian concept of adoption through Christ |
| ✗      | No personal Creator-God relationship as a central doctrine                                            |

## The Five Questions

1. **Who is God?**
2. **What is humanity's problem?**
3. **How is salvation or liberation achieved?**
4. **Who is Jesus?**
5. **What is humanity's ultimate destiny?**
