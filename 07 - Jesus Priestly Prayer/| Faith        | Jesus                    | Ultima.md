## Jesus and Humanity's Ultimate Destiny

| Faith        | Jesus                           | Ultimate Destiny                     | Rel. |
| ------------ | ------------------------------- | ------------------------------------ | ---- |
| Judaism      | not divine; not Messiah         | World to Come                        | ~    |
| Christianity | Son of God, Savior, Lord        | Eternal life with God                | ✓    |
| Islam        | Prophet and Messiah, not divine | Paradise or Hell                     | ~    |
| Hinduism     | Teacher/avatar (varies)         | Moksha                               | ~    |
| Buddhism     | Enlightened teacher             | Nirvana                              | ✗    |
| Sikhism      | Great teacher; not divine       | Union with God; freedom from rebirth | ~    |

### Key

| Symbol | Meaning                                                                                               |
| ------ | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| ✓      | Personal relationship with God through Christ as adopted children of God                              |
| ~      | Relationship with God/divine reality exists, but not the Christian concept of adoption through Christ |
| ✗      | No personal Creator-God relationship as a central doctrine                                            |
