One way to view the outline is through the signs Jesus performs, which reveal his glory. John presents seven key signs: 
	1.) the changing of water into wine (John 2:1-11)
	2.) the healing of the official's son (John 4:43-54)
	3.) the healing at the pool of Bethesda (John 5:1-15)
	4.) the feeding of the five thousand (John 6:1-14)
	5.) Jesus walking on water (John 6:16-21) 
	6.) the healing of the man born blind (John 9:1-41)
	7.) and the raising of Lazarus (John 11:1-44).
