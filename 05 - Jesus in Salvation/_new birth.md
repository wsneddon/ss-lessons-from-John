# New Birth, New Creation, and Regeneration

**The Theme of Spiritual Renewal in the Bible**

The Bible presents salvation as far more than forgiveness of sins. It is a **new birth**, a **new creation**, and **regeneration** — God making us spiritually alive with a new heart and new nature.

---

### Key Greek Terms (New Testament)

| English Concept         | Greek                          | Transliteration         | Meaning |
|-------------------------|--------------------------------|-------------------------|---------|
| Born again / from above | γεννηθῇ ἄνωθεν                | *gennēthē anōthen*      | Born again or born from above |
| Born of God             | γεγέννηται ἐκ τοῦ θεοῦ         | *gegennētai ek tou theou* | Begotten of God |
| Born again (verb)       | ἀναγεννάω                     | *anagennaō*             | To beget again, cause to be born again |
| Regeneration            | παλιγγενεσία                  | *palingenesia*          | Rebirth, regeneration, new genesis |
| New creation            | καινὴ κτίσις                  | *kainē ktisis*          | New creation |
| Made alive together     | συζωοποιέω                    | *syzōopoieō*            | Made alive together (with Christ) |

### New Birth / Born Again
**John 1:12–13**
> “…to all who did receive him… he gave the right to become children of God, who were born, **not of blood** nor of the will of the flesh nor of the will of man, **but of God**.”

**John 3:3–8** (Jesus to Nicodemus)
> “Unless one is **born again** [γεννηθῇ ἄνωθεν] he cannot see the kingdom of God… That which is born of the Spirit is spirit.”

**1 Peter 1:3**
> “He has caused us to be **born again** [ἀναγεννάω] to a living hope through the resurrection of Jesus Christ…”

**1 Peter 1:23**
> “Having been born again, not of perishable seed but of imperishable, through the living and abiding word of God.”

**1 John** repeatedly emphasizes:
- “Everyone who believes… has been born of God” (5:1)
- “No one born of God makes a practice of sinning” (3:9)
- “Everyone who loves has been born of God” (4:7)

## Old Testament Foundations

These promises show God’s plan to transform His people from the inside out.

### Heart of Stone → Heart of Flesh
**Ezekiel 36:25–27**
> “I will sprinkle clean water on you, and you shall be clean… And I will give you **a new heart**, and a **new spirit** I will put within you. And I will remove the **heart of stone** from your flesh and give you a **heart of flesh**. And I will put my Spirit within you, and cause you to walk in my statutes…”

**Ezekiel 11:19–20**  
> “I will give them one heart and a new spirit I will put within them. I will remove the heart of stone from their flesh and give them a heart of flesh…”

### New Covenant: Law Written on the Heart
**Jeremiah 31:31–34**
> “Behold, the days are coming… when I will make a **new covenant**… I will put my law within them, and I will write it on their hearts… I will forgive their iniquity, and I will remember their sin no more.”

**Jeremiah 32:39–40**  
> “I will give them one heart and one way… I will put the fear of me in their hearts…”

### Circumcision of the Heart
**Deuteronomy 30:6**
> “And the Lord your God will **circumcise your heart**… so that you will love the Lord your God with all your heart…”

**Deuteronomy 10:16**  
> “Circumcise therefore the foreskin of your heart…”

### Other Key Passages
- **Psalm 51:10** — “Create in me a clean heart, O God, and renew a right spirit within me.”
- **Jeremiah 24:7** — “I will give them a heart to know that I am the Lord…”

### Regeneration & Renewal
**Titus 3:5**
> “He saved us… by the washing of **regeneration** [παλιγγενεσία] and renewal of the Holy Spirit.”

---

### New Creation
**2 Corinthians 5:17**
> “If anyone is in Christ, **he is a new creation** [καινὴ κτίσις]. The old has passed away; behold, the new has come.”

**Galatians 6:15**
> “Neither circumcision nor uncircumcision means anything; what counts is **a new creation**.”

---

### Made Alive with Christ
**Ephesians 2:1–10**
> “You were **dead** in the trespasses and sins… But God… made us **alive together with Christ**… For we are his workmanship, created in Christ Jesus for good works.”

**Colossians 2:13**
> “You, who were dead in your sins… God made you alive together with him.”

**Romans 6:4**
> “Just as Christ was raised from the dead… we too might walk in **newness of life**.”

## The Great Biblical Pattern

| Old Condition              | New Reality in Christ               |
|----------------------------|-------------------------------------|
| Heart of stone             | Heart of flesh / New heart          |
| Uncircumcised heart        | Circumcised heart                   |
| External law               | Law written on the heart            |
| Physical birth (Adam)      | Spiritual birth (born of God)       |
| Spiritual death            | Made alive with Christ              |
| Old creation               | New creation                        |
| Human effort               | God’s sovereign grace               |

---

**Summary**

Salvation is:
- A **new birth** from above
- A **new heart** and **new spirit**
- **Regeneration** by the Holy Spirit
- A complete **new creation**

This transformation is entirely God’s work, received by faith, and results in a new life of love, obedience, and holiness.
